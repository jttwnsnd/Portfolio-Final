Blackjack Challenges

Keeping track of card values is easy until you get to anything Jacks and above. Aces were exceptionally tricky. To help keep track, giving conditionals to control the value is what I found to keep the values in check. With Aces being the special case, I used if((cardValue == 11) && (total > 21)) as my conditional to check whether Ace needs to be 1 or 11, creating a dynamic and realistic feel to the game.
